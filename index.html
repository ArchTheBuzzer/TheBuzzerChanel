About The Buzzer Channel

The Buzzer Channel is a YouTube channel dedicated to exploring the fascinating world of shortwave radio and mysterious broadcasts. Join us as we delve into the enigma of UVB-76 and uncover the secrets of Radiofax transmissions.

Shortwave Radio

Shortwave radio refers to the radio frequencies in the HF (high frequency) range. It is used for long-distance communication and can be received over vast distances due to its ability to reflect off the ionosphere.

UVB-76

UVB-76, also known as "The Buzzer", is a mysterious shortwave radio station that has been broadcasting since the late 1970s. It is known for its continuous buzzing sound and occasional voice messages in Russian.

Radiofax

Radiofax, also known as weatherfax, is a method of transmitting weather charts and images via radio. It is used by ships and aircraft to receive weather information when out of range of traditional communication methods.
